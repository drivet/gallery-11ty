Hello! My name is Desmond Rivet and this is my photo gallery.  Though I have
a [blog](https://desmondrivet.com/posts/) where I occasionally post
off-the-cuff photos, this site represents a more curated, organized
experience, where I try my best to choose photos that go well together, and
where I put some effort into editing the content before publication.

I'm not a great photographer but I do, in my opinion, occasionally take a
good shot.  Enjoy the site.
