Hello! My name is Desmond Rivet and this is my photo gallery.  Though I also
have a [blog](https://desmondrivet.com/posts/) where I occasionally post
off-the-cuff photos, this site is a bit more curated and organized, where I
try my best to choose photos that go well together, and where I put some
effort into editing the content before publication.  I'm not a great
photographer but I do, in my opinion, occasionally take a good shot.

Though I've jokingly referred to this site as a "display case" in my head,
it's actually less about showcasing my (mediocre) photos and more having a
convenient way of sharing them with friends and family.
